Prototypes

When I started my Computer Games Design and Programming course at Staffordshire University in September of 2018, I had 0 experience making video games.

What I had plenty of though, was a drive to learn.
So I got to work.

During my 3 years in university, I developed a number of video game prototypes, a diverse collection of ideas and concepts that showcase my creativity and technical skills.

I am constantly experimenting with new ideas and technologies, and I am always looking for ways to push the boundaries of what is possible in the world of video games. These prototypes showcase my passion for innovation and my desire to create truly memorable gaming experiences.

I am proud of the work I have put into my video game prototypes, and I am excited to share them with you. I hope you will find something in my prototypes that speaks to you.
